Splash into Summer

Splash Pad at the Pearl Brewery, July 22, 2018

Beat the San Antonio heat with a nice cold Kambucha from Element at the Farmers Market.

The fun starts with the splash-pad for the kiddos of all ages. They love the fresh cold water splashing their face. There is also plenty of free water given at the Bottling Company to stay hydrated!

Eat Gelato - It's Amore!

South Alamode Gelato, July 2, 2018

With the summer at it's peak all you want to do is lounge indoors with some ice cream.

I have found a great place for you! It's indoors, has the best gelato in town and they have board games that will help you forget about the dreadful heat outdoors.
