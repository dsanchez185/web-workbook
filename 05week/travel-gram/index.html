Travel-Gram
San Antonio Edition

San Antonio's well traveled grandma loves watching the sunset in her city. She views the skyline through her condo window where colors burst into shades of pink, orange and yellow.

Grandma loves to visit the historical Mission - The Alamo. Her favorite place to admire the monument is in the near by gazebo, where she sits and feeds the pigeons.

Age is no barrier when it comes to travel...

Beer, bacon and bingo are grandma's go to fun-times at Alamo Brewery.
